WELCOME TO GO GLOBAL FOUNDATION

Our foundation enhances youth opportunities by advancing STEM education, providing career support, and addressing global challenges like climate change and poverty.

ABOUT US

Our foundation is dedicated to promoting education and opportunities for young people around the world. Our work is centered around three main focus areas: STEM education, career development and global issues solving.
Our STEM education initiative is designed to inspire and engage young people in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We believe that these fields are critical to solving many of the world's most pressing problems. As a foundation, we strive to connect the lessons taught in classrooms to the vast realm of life outside. We seek to enrich students' educational experiences by showcasing real-life applications of the theories they learn.
Our career development initiative enlightens young people through workshops, mentorship programs, and exposure to various career paths, empowering these young minds to make informed decisions about their professional journeys. We recognize that finding the right career path can be challenging, and we are committed to providing mentorship programs to job fairs, offering a range of services to encourage young people in achieving their career goals.
Finally, our global issues solving initiative is focused on addressing some of the world's most pressing problems. From climate change to poverty to human rights violations, there are many complex issues facing our world today especially the UN Sustainable development goals. We believe that young people have an important role to play in solving these problems, and empower them to make positive change and a difference in their environment.
We are proud to focus our efforts on these three main areas at GO Global Foundation. We believe that we have the potential to make a real difference in the lives of young people around the world.

OUR VISION:

Inspiring and awakening the transformation of lives.

OUR MISSION:

To inspire communities by influencing, educating and improving our environment with positive change and value.
