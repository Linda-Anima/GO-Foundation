ABOUT US

Our foundation is dedicated to promoting education and opportunities for young people around the world. Our work is centered around three main focus areas: STEM education, career development and global issues solving.
Our STEM education initiative is designed to inspire and engage young people in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We believe that these fields are critical to solving many of the world's most pressing problems.
As a foundation, we strive to connect the lessons taught in classrooms to the vast realm of life outside. We seek to enrich students' educational experiences by showcasing real-life applications of the theories they learn.
Finally, our global issues solving initiative is focused on addressing some of the world's most pressing problems. From climate change to poverty to human rights violations, there are many complex issues facing our world today especially the UN Sustainable development goals.
We believe that young people have an important role to play in solving these problems, and empower them to make positive change and a difference in their environment.
We are proud to focus our efforts on these three main areas at GO Global Foundation. We believe that we have the potential to make a real difference in the lives of young people around the world.
By fostering innovation, leadership, and a sense of responsibility, we equip the next generation with the skills and confidence they need to tackle the challenges of today and shape a brighter, more sustainable tomorrow.

SERVICES

Workshops and Mentorship

Our career development initiative enlightens young people through workshops, mentorship programs, and exposure to various career paths, empowering these young minds to make informed decisions about their professional journeys.

Support for Career Decisions

We recognize that finding the right career path can be challenging, and we are committed to providing mentorship programs to job fairs, offering a range of services to encourage young people in achieving their career goals.

Bridging Education and Workforce

By bridging the gap between education and the workforce, we aim to equip youth with the tools, connections, and confidence they need to thrive in a rapidly evolving job market.

Inspiring Resilience and Adaptability

Ultimately, we strive to inspire resilience and adaptability, helping them turn their ambitions into achievable realities. We aim to prepare them for both challenges and opportunities in their future careers.
